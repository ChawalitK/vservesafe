Note:

When Cooling Foods, they should be cooled from above 57C to at or below 5C within 6 hours from start of the process.

Divide the cooked food into shallow pans or smaller pots. Place them in ice bath and stir frequently to facilitate rapid cooling.


Instructions:

0 hr - means starting food temperature

1 to 4 hours - means time taken to cool

Type of container use - means food container used during cooling process
